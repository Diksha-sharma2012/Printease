ABOUT HUGLI PRINTING PRESS

Innovative printing solutions for modern businesses since 2010

Quality Guaranteed

Your Trusted Printing Partner

Founded in 2010, Hugli Printing Press has grown from a small local print shop to one of North India's most trusted printing service providers. Our journey has been marked by consistent innovation, uncompromising quality, and a customer-first approach that has earned us long-term partnerships with businesses across multiple industries.

With state-of-the-art printing technology and a team of skilled craftsmen, we bridge the gap between traditional printing excellence and modern digital innovation. Our facility in Barnala houses some of the most advanced printing equipment in the region, including Komori offset presses and Xerox digital presses.

Our Printing Expertise

Comprehensive solutions for all your printing needs

Die Cut Business Cards

As India's leading Die Cut Card Manufacturer, we specialize in precision-cut visiting cards that make your brand stand out. Our proprietary cutting technology ensures flawless edges every time.

Premium Business Cards

From luxurious velvet finishes to sleek PVC cards, our premium range offers unmatched quality. Choose from matt, gloss, embossed, or specialty finishes to perfectly represent your brand.

Leaflets & Posters

Produced on our presses using premium quality paper, our marketing materials deliver vibrant colors and sharp details that capture attention and communicate your message effectively.

Stickers & Labels

Our extensive sticker range includes options with half-cut precision and protective lamination, perfect for product labeling, promotions, and brand identification.

ATM Pouches

The innovative alternative to traditional business cards, our ATM pouches combine functionality with brand visibility. Minimum order quantity of just 1000 units makes them accessible for businesses of all sizes.

Garment Tags

With minimum orders starting at 2000 pieces, we offer apparel tags in various sizes and premium quality options, helping clothing brands maintain a professional image at competitive prices.

Our Vision

To revolutionize India's printing industry by creating a unified platform that empowers printers nationwide. We aim to foster collaboration, innovation, and collective growth while extending welfare initiatives to support printing professionals across the country.

Our Mission

To establish India as a global leader in printing technology through continuous innovation. We're committed to developing cutting-edge printing solutions that serve both domestic and international markets while maintaining sustainable business practices.

Our Promise

Exclusive B2B services tailored for printers and advertising agencies. We guarantee premium quality, strict adherence to timelines, and transparent pricing that delivers exceptional value. Our no-compromise approach ensures you always receive products that exceed expectations.
